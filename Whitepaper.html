Gen-Z Taking Over Metaverse


Jakarta, April 26th 2022 - Metaverse is a buzzword not strange to the ears of many. To put it in simple terms, it allows us to experience the real world through a virtual one without any frames. Metaverse has endless opportunities in, especially business, economics, education, IT, and entertainment. The youth of now particularly endears or is at least not unfamiliar about co-existing with the virtual world, hence creating demand for digital experiences such as the one offered by the Metaverse.

Advisia together with WIR Global launched a White Paper Project which aims to better understand how the perception of Gen Z on the metaverse together with exploring its optimal usability with the aim to generate actionable insights for the enterprise.

The research was conducted by taking samples from 194 respondents aged 18-24 years. This study shows that as many as 62.9% of respondents have an interest in ownership of virtual reality devices. Meanwhile, 3.76% of respondents already have a visual reality tool. From the results of this study, 69.35% of Indonesian youth that are surveyed showed a positive attitude towards Metaverse and 65.81% of respondents are willing to spend money on Metaverse.

Metaverse is expected to bring progress in various sectors, including real estate, education, financial services, retail, entertainment, automotive, and consumer goods, among others.

To better capitalize on the evolving future, a robust set of extensive assessments and fact-based approaches are required to develop strategies to pave entry to the Metaverse ecosystem and market. With the right market knowledge and operational expertise, Advisia can be your partner to to leverage the current favorable demographics together with the technical and legal environments to successfully break-into Metaverse.
